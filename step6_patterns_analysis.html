Step 6 — Geographic & Temporal Patterns Analysis¶

Goal: Using the flood articles identified by our classifier, examine:

  1. Where floods are most reported — which divisions get the most coverage?
  2. When floods are reported — seasonal and yearly trends
  3. Who reports them — do different newspapers cover different regions?
  4. Policy angle — compare news coverage against actual damage records (deaths, affected people)

Data used:

  • timeseries_data/yearPublished_year_month/ — monthly flood article counts by division
  • timeseries_data/yearPublished_day_newspaper/ — daily flood article counts by newspaper
  • other_data/emdat_flood.csv — EM-DAT global disaster database (deaths, affected)
  • other_data/Damage data_Hassan.csv — Red Cross Bangladesh damage data
  • other_data/Flood_Affected_Area_Barchart.csv — Government flood area data
